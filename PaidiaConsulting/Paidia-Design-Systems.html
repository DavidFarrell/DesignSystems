Signature motifs

Rules of use

Do

  • Reserve yellow for ONE element per viewport
  • Push display type to viewport-edge sizes
  • Keep cards on a single ink-raised tone (#181818)
  • Use mono only for metadata, never body

Don't

  • Mix Archivo Black with another display face
  • Round corners above 24px on cards
  • Use yellow on long-form body copy

Signature motifs

Hot magenta as a highlight slab behind a single word in display type — never as standalone text under 24px. Numeric section markers in italic ("01., 02."). Asymmetric two-column grid: short content column + long content column, alternating.

Rules of use

Do

  • Use magenta as a slab/highlight behind one word
  • Keep grid asymmetric — never 50/50
  • Pair Archivo Black headlines with Archivo body
  • Wrap callouts in lemon-cream when softer pop is needed

Don't

  • Use magenta on body copy
  • Centre body text — always left-aligned
  • Put magenta on cream without ink

Signature motifs

Slight rotations (±2°) on cards and buttons to imply hand-printing. Two riso "spot colours" — yellow and pink — used as full tile fills, never blended. Halftone dot patterns acceptable as background texture.

Rules of use

Do

  • Use yellow OR pink, rarely both in the same card
  • Keep rotations subtle (±2° max)
  • Use full-bleed riso tiles for stats

Don't

  • Add a third or fourth riso colour
  • Rotate body copy (only display + chrome)
  • Use gradients — riso is flat-fill only

Signature motifs

2×2 metric-tile grid in the hero (clay + lemon + sky + ink). Ink slabs behind one word in the headline ("actually"). Italic numerals as section markers (01., 02.). Soft rounded corners (10–18px) — never sharp.

Rules of use

Do

  • Use all three blocks (clay/lemon/sky) per spread
  • Keep ink as the anchor — at least one ink element per section
  • Italic Fraunces for display, Geist for body

Don't

  • Use Fraunces for body copy (italic gets tiring)
  • Add a fourth accent — three blocks max
  • Use sky on text smaller than 24px (low contrast)

CASE-STUDY ROW
No. 02 — GOSH Charity
70% adoption · 12 weeks

Possibly the fastest charity AI transformation in the UK.

Signature motifs

Coral italic drop-caps opening every body section. Hairline rules separating numbered case rows. Big stat strip (3-up) bordered top + bottom. Pull-quote section in solid teal with coral highlight slab on key word.

Rules of use

Do

  • Open every long article with a drop cap
  • Keep two-column reading width (max 60ch)
  • Use teal as background for one section per page

Don't

  • Centre body — always left-aligned
  • Use coral and teal in the same word
  • Add a third accent — only teal + coral
