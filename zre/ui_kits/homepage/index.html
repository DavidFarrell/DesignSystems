About ZINC Real Estate

A specialist asset manager with a single discipline.

We pair a dynamic capital allocation framework with committed capital, operational agility and empowered teams - across UK offices, European logistics and selective residential opportunities.

Our partners and investment team have managed real estate through three cycles. The firm was founded on the principle that operational depth - not financial engineering - is what compounds returns over a hold.
