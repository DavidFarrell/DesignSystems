Two families. The discipline is the design.

Reservation Wide carries every label and every headline. Plantin carries every paragraph. There is no third family - no monospace, no geometric sans, no "modern" cut for buttons. The pairing is the brand.

Body · 18px / 1.7 (Light)
At Zinc, we are driven to deliver exceptional returns for our valued investors. Committed to a relentless pursuit of excellence. The default body weight is Plantin Light, set generously, so a long page reads like a printed brochure rather than a UI screen.
Body · Roman (400)
Roman is reserved for emphasis runs and for pull quotes that need slightly more weight than Light.
Body · Semibold (600)
Semibold is rare - reserved for the lead sentence of a contact card or an in-line emphasis.
Italic Plantin
Italic Plantin Light carries person roles (Partner | CEO) and figure captions.
