Preview · Brand voice

Discreet. Specific. Quietly Assertive.

ZRE speaks like a private bank's letter to its oldest client. Sentences are short and declarative. Numbers do most of the work. The site never raises its voice and never asks for anything except a contact form.

Casing

  • Headlines - Title Case in Reservation Wide Bold.
  • Eyebrows - ALL CAPS, TRACKED 0.18EM in Reservation Wide.
  • Buttons - VIEW OUR INVESTMENTS - never sentence case.
  • Body - sentence case in Plantin Light, British English.
  • People names - BOB VAN DEN BICHELAER / Partner | CEO.

Exemplars (lifted from the live site)

"At Zinc, we are driven to deliver exceptional returns for our valued investors."
"Committed to a relentless pursuit of excellence."
"We pair a dynamic capital allocation framework with committed capital, operational agility and empowered teams."
"Assets under management - £600m."

Do / don't

Do
  • Lead every section with a Title-Case Reservation headline.
  • Keep the palette to forest green + cream + grey.
  • Desaturate portrait photography to true black-and-white.
  • Let numbers carry the proof - £600m, 35+ years, 9 offices.
Don't
  • Use "synergy", "ecosystem", "unlock", "best-in-class".
  • Introduce a second brand colour. Use opacity or grey instead.
  • Apply drop shadows. Use a hairline rule or a deeper green.
  • Pair Plantin with another serif, or Reservation with another display.
