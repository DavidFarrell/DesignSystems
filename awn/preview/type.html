One typeface. Six weights. Hierarchy through scale, not contrast.

The family

Awin's site uses Inter (Rasmus Andersson) for everything - display, body, UI chrome, button labels. Six cuts are loaded; semibold (600) carries the bulk of the body emphasis, with bold (700) reserved for headlines.

Specimen paragraph

Awin connects 30K+ trusted brands and 1M+ approved partners. The voice is friendly and confident - not startup-shouty. Sentences are short, headlines lean on subject + verb, and emphasis lives in semibold rather than colour. Inline links use the signature orange and underline at 3px offset.
