Editorial Mono

A Swiss editorial system for thingsithinkithink — a working notebook from a software-and-design studio. Three families. One accent. Sharp corners.

SET BY DAVID GÉROUVILLE-FARRELL

What this is

Reads like a working notebook: hierarchy through space and weight, not boxes and color. Mono carries structure - labels, captions, indices. Serif carries prose. Sans carries UI chrome. Vermilion is the only voice raised above the page, and it appears in exactly five places: the section glyph §, the drop cap on the first paragraph, link underlines, the published-state dot, and prev/next chevrons. Nowhere else.
